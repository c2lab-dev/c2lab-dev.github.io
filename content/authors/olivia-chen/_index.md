Associate Professor at Kyushu University, directing the C2Lab (Cryogenic Computing Laboratory). Research focuses on end-to-end superconducting digital computing systems.

Olivia Chen is an Associate Professor at Kyushu University and the Director of the **C2Lab (Cryogenic Computing Laboratory)**. Her research focuses on **end-to-end superconducting digital computing systems**, spanning from device technologies and circuit architecture to full-stack EDA workflows for scalable cryogenic systems.
